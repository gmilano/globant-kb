## LATAM Market Analysis

### Developer Ecosystem (2026)
- Brazil: ~900k developers, 2nd largest GitHub contributor base in Americas after USA
- Mexico: ~700k developers, strong nearshore tech hub for US companies
- Argentina: ~500k developers, highest per-capita GitHub activity in LATAM
- Colombia: ~280k developers, fastest-growing tech sector in region
- Chile: ~180k developers, fintech and startup hub

### LATAM Opportunity Areas
1. **AI-augmented nearshore teams**: LATAM dev shops offering "AI-accelerated delivery" as differentiator
2. **Open source preference**: Cost sensitivity drives OSS adoption — LangChain, FastAPI, MLflow over paid tools
3. **Lagging MLOps adoption**: Only ~15% of LATAM companies have mature MLOps; Globant opportunity to accelerate
4. **Spanish-language AI tooling gap**: Most agent frameworks, docs, tutorials still English-only
5. **Cloud migration wave**: 60%+ of LATAM enterprises still on-prem; cloud migration + AI modernization bundled

### Competitive Landscape in LATAM
- **Accenture, IBM**: Largest competitors, strong in enterprise transformation
- **Softtek, Wizeline**: Mexican nearshore firms adding AI practices
- **Atos, Capgemini**: European players with LATAM presence
- **Globant Advantage**: AI Studios model, strong brand in tech sector, existing relationships with gaming/media/tech clients
