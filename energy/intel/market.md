## LATAM Energy Market

### Brazil — Largest LATAM Market
- **Grid operator**: ONS (Operador Nacional do Sistema Eletrico)
- **Renewable share**: >85% hydro + wind + solar (2026)
- **Key players**: Eletrobras, CPFL, Equatorial, Neoenergia, Eneva
- **2026 signal**: National smart meter rollout started — 3.6M meters, 4% of customers year 1 (Ministry of Mines and Energy). CPFL rollout began March 2026.
- **Smart grid market**: $1.58B (2024) → $5.8B (2033), 10% CAGR
- **AI opportunity**: Smart grid modernization, BESS optimization, EV charging fleet, theft detection
- **Regulatory**: ANEEL VPP framework Q4 2026 — first VPP market in LATAM

### Chile — Renewable Leader
- **Grid operator**: Coordinador Electrico Nacional (CEN)
- **Renewable share**: >50% renewable (solar+wind+hydro, 2026)
- **Key players**: Enel Chile, AES Andes, Colbun, Engie Chile
- **AI opportunity**: Mining electrification (copper/lithium), solar curtailment reduction, H2 verde
- **Highlight**: Chile's copper/lithium mines consume ~33% of national electricity — massive industrial energy optimization opportunity
- **Data**: CEN open data portal provides high-quality historical data for AI training

### Colombia — Emerging Market
- **Grid operator**: XM (Centro Nacional de Despacho)
- **Renewable share**: ~70% hydro; wind+solar growing from ~1% to 12% target by 2030
- **Key players**: Ecopetrol (energy transition), Celsia, EPM, Isagen
- **AI opportunity**: Hydro dispatch optimization, demand forecasting, solar integration
- **Highlight**: Ecopetrol announced LLM pilot for refinery optimization 2026

### Argentina
- **Grid operator**: CAMMESA
- **Key players**: YPF (YPF Luz renewables), Pampa Energia, Central Puerto
- **AI opportunity**: Grid efficiency under infrastructure stress, smart metering AI
- **Globant angle**: YPF home-country client; YPF Luz solar farm expansion
- **Challenge**: FX instability complicates SaaS pricing; prefer fixed-scope professional services

### Mexico
- **Grid operator**: CENACE
- **Renewable share**: ~25%; CFE dominance constrains growth
- **Key players**: CFE, Iberdrola Mexico, Enel Green Power Mexico
- **AI opportunity**: T&D loss reduction (>15% current losses), predictive maintenance, demand response
- **Highlight**: LATAM data center AI infrastructure investment boom (Jul 2026) — data center energy optimization emerging
