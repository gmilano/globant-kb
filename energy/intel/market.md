### LATAM First-Mover Gaps (No Incumbent as of July 2026)

1. **Spanish-language grid operator chatbot** — OperatorFabric + Claude for Spanish-speaking TSO/DSO operators. Zero commercial competition.
2. **Eletrobras-pattern at other LATAM utilities** — C3 AI Grid Intelligence validated the use case; open-source equivalent (VOLTTRON + LangGraph) is achievable for smaller utilities without C3 AI licensing cost.
3. **PIX/PSE-integrated demand response** — Link BESS dispatch decisions to local energy market settlements. No open solution exists.
4. **PyPSA LATAM Reference Models** — PyPSA-Eur is the gold standard for Europe; no equivalent for LATAM. First mover wins mindshare.
5. **EV Charging AI for LATAM fleet operators** — EVerest + PowerMCP smart charging agent; fleet operators (Rappi, LATAM Airlines, bus operators) are EV-transitioning with no charging AI.
6. **PowerMCP rapid PoC for LATAM utilities** — Utilities with OpenDSS (standard in LATAM distribution planning) can have an LLM-driven grid analysis PoC in 2-3 weeks. Globant can be the delivery partner.
7. **NEW: AI Grid Management for Renewable Curtailment (Chile/Brazil)** — Chile at 63% renewable electricity; managing curtailment and grid stability requires AI. No LATAM-specific solution exists — PyPSA + LangGraph + Claude.
8. **NEW: AI Data Center Grid Integration (Santiago/Rio/CDMX)** — LATAM AI data center boom is creating grid pressure. Utilities need AI grid management tools. Opportunity to serve both data center operators AND utilities.
