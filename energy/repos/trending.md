## Breakout New Repos (2026)

### Power-Agent Ecosystem (Harvard SEAS)
**`github.com/Power-Agent`** — entire organization trending

The Harvard SEAS PowerAgent group released 4 coordinated repos in H1 2026:
- `PowerMCP` — MCP servers for simulators
- `PowerDAG` — agentic distribution grid analysis
- `PowerFM` — foundation models registry
- `PowerWF` — agentic workflow library
- `PowerSkills` — agent skills for power engineering tasks

All MIT licensed. Collectively the most coherent agentic energy framework available.

### agentic-ai-hems
**LLM-based home energy management** — Growing quickly as utilities explore residential AI

Unique value: transparent ReAct reasoning traces (critical for EU AI Act compliance in consumer-facing energy AI). Template adoptable by any Globant energy engagement.

### GridMind — Argonne National Laboratory
**LLM + solver co-pilot** — Lab credibility driving rapid adoption

Production path: utility deploys GridMind as control room co-pilot → operators query in natural language → deterministic solvers provide answers → GridMind interprets and recommends.
