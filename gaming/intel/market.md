# Market Map — AI in Gaming (2026)

## Market Size

- **$22 billion** profit opportunity from AI tooling by 2026 (Morgan Stanley Research)
- AI in Games market growing at significant CAGR through 2033
- **80%+** of gaming companies using generative AI for content and insights (State of AI in Gaming 2026)
- Agentic AI adoption still **<20%** — constrained by compliance and player safety concerns — representing a greenfield opportunity

## Globant Positioning

**Sweet spot:** Enterprise gaming studios and publishers who want:
1. Open-source AI NPC systems they can audit and ship on-device
2. AI-accelerated asset pipelines that cut production cost 30–45%
3. ML-driven matchmaking and anti-cheat integrated into their existing Nakama/custom backends
4. Autonomous game QA / playtesting bots (RL-based) replacing manual testers

**Competitive moat:** Globant can deliver the full stack — engine integration, AI agent training, backend infra, and MLOps — that boutique AI gaming startups cannot.
