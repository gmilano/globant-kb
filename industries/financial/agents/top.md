## Notes for Globant Engagements

- **FinRL + FinGPT + FinRobot** form a coherent AI4Finance stack — all Apache 2.0 / MIT, production-ready
- **ai-hedge-fund** is the fastest entry point for a multi-agent demo (50K stars, active community)
- **Rasa** is the go-to for bank/insurance customer service chatbots needing on-premise deployment
- **OpenBB** is replacing Bloomberg Terminal workflows at quant shops — extensible with custom agents
- **QuantLib** is essential for any derivatives/fixed-income pricing work (30+ years of math, BSD licensed)
