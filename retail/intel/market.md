## Competitive Dynamics 2026

### Agentic Commerce Race
Google's Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is reshaping the industry — AI agents shopping on behalf of consumers will disintermediate traditional search-and-browse. Retailers need:
- Machine-readable product APIs (structured data, JSON-LD)
- UCP-compatible checkout flows
- Agent-friendly pricing and inventory availability feeds

**Winners:** Medusa and Saleor (early UCP movers); headless-native platforms
**Losers:** Closed platforms without public APIs; SEO-dependent traffic models

### Cloud vs Self-Hosted Tension
34% of mid-market retailers now prefer self-hosted AI (recommendation, forecasting) after cloud cost pressures from 2024-2025. Gorse, NeuralForecast, and Apache PredictionIO are the primary beneficiaries.

### Physical Retail Resurgence
Physical stores are gaining share as "trust-building hubs" — AI-augmented in-store experiences (smart shelves, associate assist, frictionless checkout) are the new battleground. Vision AI at the edge is growing rapidly.
