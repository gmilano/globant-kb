## Quick context

Legal is one of the highest-value AI verticals in 2026: 79% of legal professionals already use AI, and firms are moving from single-tool experimentation to multi-agent workflow automation. The biggest near-term opportunities are contract review/CLM, legal research, compliance monitoring, and document intelligence — all of which have strong open-source foundations.

**Preferred licenses for Globant engagements**: MIT, Apache 2.0. Avoid AGPL-3.0 unless wrapping in a service layer.
