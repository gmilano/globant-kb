## Key Milestone: "March 2026 — The Month Agentic Travel Gets Real"

OAG (aviation analytics) published analysis naming **March 2026** as the inflection point:
- Google agentic booking integration live (AI Mode) with Booking.com, Expedia, Choice Hotels, IHG, Marriott, Wyndham
- First measurable autonomous transactions at scale (not just pilots)
- Corporate enterprise crossing the production threshold

This date marks the transition from "agentic travel is coming" to "agentic travel is here."

## Globant Opportunity Map

### Near-Term (0-6 months) — Highest ROI
1. **LATAM Hotel AI Concierge (WhatsApp)** — QloApps/HotelDruid + Claude + WhatsApp. Compete with AskSuite/Visito. Target: LATAM independent hotels and chains.
2. **Corporate Travel Automation** — Amadeus SDK + policy enforcement. Entry: `travel-booking-agents` pattern. Compete with Navan/SAP Concur.
3. **Tour Operator Digitization** — ExcursioX + AI + WhatsApp. LATAM operators stuck on spreadsheets + WhatsApp manually. High-volume, underserved.

### Medium-Term (6-18 months) — Platform Play
4. **OTA AI Backbone** — Help existing OTAs integrate agentic AI to compete with Google AI Mode. Amadeus + Duffel MCP wrappers as the integration layer.
5. **Travel Data Infrastructure** — Clean data pipelines (PMS → unified schema → agent API). McKinsey: "Winners are mastering the boring back end." Offer AI readiness audits.
6. **NDC Integration Layer** — Help airlines/hotels connect to NDC + expose as agent-accessible APIs. Critical for Duffel-based agentic booking.

### Long-Term (18+ months) — Consumer AI
7. **Consumer Agentic Booking Platform** — Wait for trust gap to narrow (currently 2-7% willing, will be 20-30% by 2028). Build the white-label platform that travel brands can deploy.
8. **Medical Tourism / MICE AI Platform** — Yatra-Vritta patterns + enterprise sales to healthcare networks and event companies.
