## Trend 1: Agentic AI Deployment (2026)
Google announced agentic booking in AI Mode (Nov 2025) with Booking.com, Expedia, Choice Hotels, IHG, Marriott, Wyndham. McKinsey published "Remapping Travel with Agentic AI." IDC: 30% of bookings by AI agents by 2030.

## Trend 2: Trust Gap Is the Real Opportunity
Only 2% of US consumers willing to let AI book without confirmation. 61% willing to delegate planning. Design pattern: **AI as co-pilot for consumer, full autopilot for corporate with policy guardrails.**

## Trend 3: MCP Becomes Travel Infrastructure
DIDA published hotel MCP server (2M+ hotels, free, MIT) — first major travel B2B on MCP. Community Google Flights MCP server. Expect Amadeus, Duffel, Booking.com MCP servers by end 2026.

## Trend 4: Agent-Native Distribution Bypasses OTAs
LetsFG: $116 savings across 5 routes vs Google Flights (Jun 2026). Duffel NDC-first API. OTA intermediary may shrink as AI agents connect directly.

## Trend 5: Data Infrastructure is the Real Differentiator
McKinsey 2026: *"Winners are mastering the boring back end. AI needs clean data."* Offer AI readiness audits + data pipeline work as first engagement.

## Trend 6: LATAM First-Mover Window
Brazil (largest LATAM market, 94% WhatsApp), Mexico (Cancun/CDMX), Argentina (domestic recovery). WhatsApp dominance (85-95%) means travel AI must be WhatsApp-native.

## Trend 7: Corporate Travel is the First Production Use Case
Policy is machine-readable. SAP Concur, Navan, Brex building AI agents. Globant entry: corporate travel agent (booking + approval + expense) on Amadeus SDK.
