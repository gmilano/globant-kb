- **86%** of students admit to using AI on assignments — academic integrity crisis
- **71%** of higher education institutions deploying adaptive learning platforms (up from 34% in 2023)
- **42%** improvement in learning outcomes from adaptive learning systems (meta-study)
- **60%** increase in student engagement from AI-driven personalization
- AI video tools reduce course production from **80+ hours → under 5 hours**
- **67%** of educators report saving 10+ hours/week using AI content tools

## LATAM Specific Signals

- **Chamilo** is the dominant LMS in LATAM (larger installed base than Open edX in Spanish-speaking markets)
- **WhatsApp penetration** in LATAM: 95%+ → WhatsApp-first study companion is more impactful than any app install
- **ENEM** (Brazil university entrance): 3.4M+ candidates/year → AI prep is a mass-market vertical
- **Conectar Igualdad** (Argentina): ~5M low-cost devices in schools — offline-capable AI is the only viable approach
- **Beca 18** (Peru): government scholarship students need affordable AI tutoring — Kolibri + local LLM is the fit
