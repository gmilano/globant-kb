## Key Recent Moves (H1 2026)

- **Waymo $16B Series D** (Feb 2026): Post-money valuation $126B. Weekly paid rides doubled from 250k (Apr 2025) to 450k+ (Dec 2025). Robotaxi is now real business.
- **BYD #1 EV globally**: 2.26M EVs sold in 2025, surpassing Tesla. Aggressively entering LATAM (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina). Bringing Chinese-style cockpit AI with them.
- **Qualcomm + Google**: Partnership for turnkey pre-integrated AAOS SDV stack on Snapdragon Digital Chassis (CES 2026). Google open-source SDV platform release planned H2 2026.
- **Eclipse Foundation**: ADL 1.0 final spec published June 2026. S-Core 1.0 on track. Eclipse SDV confirmed #1 community priority at Eclipse World 2026.
- **Comma.ai openpilot v0.11.x**: New driving model and DM model. 325+ supported vehicles. Real-road ADAS remains the only open-source model at this scale.
- **OpenDriveVLA accepted at AAAI 2026**: First open VLA for end-to-end autonomous driving. Signals paradigm shift from modular to unified AV architectures.
- **DriveX Workshop @ CVPR 2026**: Foundation model papers for autonomous driving confirm VLA/E2E models are the industry's research frontier.
